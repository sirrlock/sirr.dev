# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 2026*

sirr.dev is the documentation website for Sirr. Here's what you should know about data and privacy.

## What we collect

We use **Sentry** for error monitoring. When something goes wrong, Sentry automatically captures:

- The error message and stack trace
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- The page URL where the error occurred

This data is used only to diagnose and fix bugs. It is not used for tracking or profiling.

## What we don't collect

- No cookies
- No personal information
- No user accounts or login data
- No analytics or page-view tracking
- No advertising or behavioral data

## Third-party services

This site is served through **Cloudflare** — their [Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/) applies to network-level data.

Error monitoring is provided by **Sentry** — their [Privacy Policy](https://sentry.io/privacy/) governs how crash data is handled.
